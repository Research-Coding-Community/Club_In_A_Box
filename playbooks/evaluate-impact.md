# Playbook: Evaluate Impact

## Goal

Collect enough evidence to improve the club, report useful outcomes, and help others reuse what works.

## Decide What Evidence Matters

Choose a small set:

- Attendance.
- Repeat attendance.
- Disciplines or roles reached.
- Topics requested.
- New helpers or facilitators.
- Materials reused.
- Clubs connected.
- Feedback themes.

## Use A Light Rhythm

- After every session: one-minute feedback.
- Monthly or termly: review attendance and topics.
- Every six months: stop/start/continue retrospective.
- Annually: public summary of what changed and what can be reused.

## Tell The Story

Impact is not just numbers. Capture:

- A new convenor taking a role.
- A group adopting a template.
- A collaboration that started at a session.
- A workflow improvement shared across teams.
- A barrier identified and removed.

## Package Outputs

For each reusable output, publish:

- What problem it solves.
- Who it is for.
- How to adapt it.
- License.
- Contact or contribution route.
