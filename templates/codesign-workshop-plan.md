## Purpose

This workshop will help us:

- Understand what is working.
- Identify barriers to participation.
- Decide which resources should be improved or created.
- Make leadership roles more visible and sustainable.
- Package learning so other clubs can reuse it.

## Participation Expectations

- Speak from your own experience.
- Make space for others.
- Avoid sharing sensitive project or personal data.
- Challenge ideas constructively.
- Help turn discussion into action.

## Activities

### 1. Community Map Review

Prompt:

- Which groups or people are missing?
- Which communities are underrepresented?
- Where is there duplication?
- Where is there isolation?

### 2. What Works

Prompt:

- Which sessions, formats, or resources have been useful?
- What helped people feel welcome?
- What should be protected?

### 3. Barriers

Prompt:

- What makes it hard to attend?
- What makes it hard to contribute?
- What makes the club hard to sustain?

### 4. Resource Prioritisation

Prompt:

- Which templates, guides, or examples would be most useful?
- What should be improved first?
- What can be removed or simplified?

### 5. Leadership Mapping

Prompt:

- Which roles exist now?
- Which roles are invisible?
- Who could shadow or rotate into a role?
- What support would make that possible?
