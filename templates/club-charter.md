# Club Charter

## Purpose

`[Club name]` is a welcoming space for people who use or want to learn coding, data, software, digital methods, reproducible research, or related technical practices.

- Share practical workflows.
- Ask questions safely.
- Build confidence.
- Find collaborators.
- Improve research and technical practice.

- `[audience]`

## How We Work

- We explain assumptions and define jargon.
- We treat questions as useful.
- We avoid sharing sensitive, private, or unpublished data in open sessions.
- We credit contributors.
- We document reusable materials where possible.
- We rotate roles so the club does not rely on one person.

## Participation Expectations

Participants agree to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Make space for others.
- Avoid gatekeeping.
- Respect confidentiality when examples involve real research.
- Follow the club code of conduct.
