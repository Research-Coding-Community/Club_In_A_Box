# Facilitator Run Sheet

Use this on the day of an event.

## Before People Arrive

- Room or link checked.
- Slides or materials open.
- Shared notes ready.
- Wi-Fi or access details visible.
- Helpers know their role.
- Registration list checked if used.
- Accessibility requests checked.
- Feedback route ready.

## Opening Script

Welcome to `[session]`. This session is for `[audience]`. Today we will `[purpose]`.

You can ask questions by `[route]`. It is fine to follow along quietly, step out, or ask for help. We will use `[shared notes link]` for links and follow-up.

## During The Session

- Keep to time.
- Repeat questions before answering.
- Check pace after each section.
- Ask helpers to support individual issues.
- Capture unresolved questions.
- Watch for people who may need a pause.

## Closing Script

We covered `[summary]`. The notes and links will be shared at `[place]`. Please leave feedback at `[link]`. Our next session is `[date or route]`.

## After The Session

- Share notes.
- Save feedback.
- Thank helpers.
- Add follow-up actions.
- Update website or calendar.
