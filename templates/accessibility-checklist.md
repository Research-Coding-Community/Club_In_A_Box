# Accessibility Checklist

## Event Information

- Event page states audience and experience level.
- Date, time, timezone, and location are clear.
- Contact route for adjustments is visible.
- Materials are available before or after the session.
- Any required software, accounts, or hardware are listed.

## Room

- Step-free access confirmed.
- Accessible toilets nearby.
- Seating can be adjusted.
- Screen is visible from all seats.
- Audio works.
- Microphone available where needed.
- Lighting is suitable.
- Break space is available if possible.

## Online Or Hybrid

- Joining link is tested.
- Captions or transcript route considered.
- Chat is monitored.
- Remote participants can ask questions.
- Shared notes are accessible.
- Screen sharing is readable.

## Catering

- Dietary requirements collected.
- Ingredients or allergens are labelled where possible.
- Food is not the only way to participate.
- Ordering matches expected numbers to reduce waste.

## Facilitation

- Acronyms will be defined.
- Questions can be asked in more than one way.
- Breaks are planned for longer sessions.
- Helpers know how to support participants.
- Feedback can be anonymous.
