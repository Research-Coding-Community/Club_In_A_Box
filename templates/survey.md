# Community Survey

Keep the survey short. Five minutes is a good target.

## Intro Text

We are mapping interest in `[club/community]` so we can plan useful sessions and avoid duplicating existing work. Responses will be reported in aggregate. Do not include sensitive data or confidential project details.

## Questions

1. Which role best describes you?
2. Which department, discipline, or team are you most connected to?
3. What technical topics would you like support with?
4. Which formats would you attend?
5. What days or times are usually possible for you?
6. What barriers make it harder for you to attend?
7. Are you already part of any related club, network, or community?
8. Would you be interested in helping run a session?
9. What would make this club useful to you?
10. Can we contact you about follow-up? If yes, provide contact details.

## Reporting

Report:

- Number of responses.
- Broad role and discipline patterns.
- Most requested topics.
- Barriers to participation.
- Existing groups identified.
- Potential helpers or convenors.
