About This Website

This website is a navigable version of the Club In A Box repository. It brings together the templates, guides, playbooks, examples, and contribution routes that help people create and sustain community-led coding and digital research clubs.

Use the sections below to open individual resources, or use the download buttons above to take a complete copy and adapt it for your own club, institution, or network.

RCC Coding Club Website Template

If your club needs its own public website, use the Research Coding Community website template. This repository provides the operating model and reusable resources, while the RCC template gives you a place to publish your club schedule, joining information, and materials.
