# Example Research Coding Club

Example Research Coding Club is a community for people who use coding, data, software, and digital methods in research.

We run practical sessions, co-working, clinics, and short demos. Beginners are welcome, and no question is too basic.

## Who Can Join

The club is open to staff, students, researchers, technical professionals, and professional services colleagues who want to learn or share digital research practice.

## How To Take Part

- Attend a session.
- Suggest a topic.
- Bring a question to a clinic.
- Share a short demo.
- Help with notes, facilitation, or the calendar.
