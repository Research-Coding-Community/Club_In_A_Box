# Acknowledgements

Club In A Box was created as part of "Building Connected Technical Networks: A Framework for Digital Communities", a project focused on strengthening digital research technical professional communities through open resources, distributed leadership, and connected local clubs.

This project has received funding through the UKRI Digital Research Infrastructure Programme via the DisCouRSE Network.

The repository draws on practical community-building needs identified through research coding clubs, digital research communities, and open research training.
